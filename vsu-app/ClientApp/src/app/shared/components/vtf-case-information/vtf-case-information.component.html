Case Information

Your application to the Victim Travel Fund (VTF) has been approved. The maximum amount of funding that can be provided in relation to a case is $3,000.00. This funding limit may be distributed over multiple criminal court/Review Board hearing dates and is distributed between eligible applicants on a case. It includes expenses pre-booked by VSU plus expenses you or other applicants may be eligible for on a reimbursement basis.

Reimbursement forms should be submitted within 90 days of your last travel dates. Where applicable, the Reimbursement form has been updated with pre-approved expenses.

Please fill out the following form to your best ability. Required receipts, if any, can be uploaded at the Declaration & Authorization stage.

Collection Notice

The Victim Services and Crime Prevention Division, Victim Safety Unit, will collect your personal information for the purpose of providing you with services and administering the Victim Travel Fund in accordance with section 26(c) of the Freedom of Information and Protection of Privacy Act (FoIPPA). Your personal information will also be collected as per section 26(e) of the FoIPPA in order to evaluate the program to better serve you. Should you have any questions about the collection of your personal information please contact the Victim Safety Unit Manager at Victim Services and Crime Prevention Division, #302-815 Hornby Street, Vancouver, BC V6Z 2E6 or by phone at (604) 660-0316 or 1-877-315-8822 (toll-free).
